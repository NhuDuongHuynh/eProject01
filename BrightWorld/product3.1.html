Product Description

The Halo Voice RL56 is an LED module with Alexa integrated directly into the fixture. It also uses the Halo Home Bluetooth Mesh technology to operate within the Halo Home system. The Voice app configures and controls the Alexa, Speaker and Microphones. The HVRL light can be controlled via the Halo Home mobile app or Halo Home in-wall Accessory Dimmers. It can also be controlled via voice when used with the Halo Home Bridge. Suitable for new construction, remodel and retrofit installation. The Halo Voice RL56 is designed to connect to the Halo Home Bluetooth Mesh network with no hub or internet connection required to control the light.
