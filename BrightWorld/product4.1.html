90W LED Floodlight RGB+W DMX WATER

The effect of the RGB+W light of this type of Floodlight is a large canyon of high power LED rays, thanks to its 90w and its harmony of colors mixed with the softness at the edges you can give a great spectacle to any type of event and in all types of spaces, especially for outside.

Characteristics:
90W: 9 leds of 10W RGB+W
RGBW 4 in 1
DMX: 4/8 channels
Operating Modes: DMX 512, Automatic, Sound Controlled, Master-Slave
Insulated cables
Connectors with screw caps.
Protection: IP65

Its efficient 9 LEDs RGBW of 10W give a superior power, and are 4 in 1 (each LED gives the 4 colors). It has automatic mode, controlled by sound, slave, or can also be controlled by DMX. With the Water 90 you will be able to illuminate the terrace of your premises, without fear of the rains, to the garden of your house. The 90 watts of power it offers allows it to be used in countless situations, as well as its IP65 protection, which allows it to be used in the most adverse weather conditions.
